What am I looking at here?

hipSpot. Find hipsters in your area.

The heatmap is displaying tweets in Greater London sent in the last seven days during the timeslot selected at the bottom of the page.

Once you have selected a venue type from the dropdown menu at the top right of the page, the map will also show you those venues that are nearby.

Particularly busy venues are marked with a star.

If you want to find hipSpots outside your current location, please use the searchbox.
